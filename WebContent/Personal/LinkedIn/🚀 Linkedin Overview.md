## Notes on Positioning

The strongest direction is not the most poetic version. It is the version that says what you do, why it matters, and where your value is hardest to replace.

The core message should stay anchored around three claims:

1. You are not only a screen designer. You work on the system underneath the screen.
2. You are strongest in ambiguous, early, messy, high-complexity product environments.
3. Your range across strategy, design systems, implementation, and AI-assisted workflows is the advantage, not a side note.

The phrase “the screen is the last step” is powerful, but it works best when used sparingly. In LinkedIn, the more recruiter-friendly version is: “Most design problems are structure problems. The screen is just where they become visible.”
