# 2. Design Philosophy & Work Style

### On Entering Problems Early
Most teams wait for a problem to be fully defined before bringing in design. That is too late. The most critical product decisions happen when the brief is still a single sentence, the roadmap is a question mark, and assumptions haven't been examined. Curiosity applied at the absolute genesis of a problem is worth ten times more than craft applied at the tail end.

### On Uncertainty as an Asset
Uncertainty isn't a variable to mitigate; it is the environment where the highest-leverage work happens. When nothing is defined, the design work is foundational thinking—establishing abstractions, framing defaults, and architecting rules. Caleb metabolizes chaos into structure fast enough for an engineering team to start moving with confidence.

### Momentum Over Perfection
A polished concept calcified inside a Figma file is decoration; a rough, operational system running live in production is a product. Caleb biases heavily toward momentum. This momentum isn't recklessness; it is driven by acute pattern recognition, high technical intuition, and a mature understanding of what a design choice costs an engineering sprint downstream.

### The Core Tactical Stack
* **Design:** Figma (System Design, Advanced Prototyping, Canvas Architecture Workflow)
* **Development:** React, TypeScript, Next.js, Tailwind CSS, Supabase, Webflow, Airtable CMS, Gemini API (`google-generativeai`)
* **Aesthetic Principle:** Light, clean, Swiss-modernist editorial typography, extreme visual hierarchy, generous negative space, minimal motion, and diagrams over superficial mockups. Inspired by clinical, authoritative restraint.

# 4. Master Portfolio Registry

## Tier 1: Deep Systems Narratives (Primary)

### 1. Major League Pickleball (MLP)
* **Role:** Sole Product Designer & Full-Stack Developer (3 Years).
* **System Architecture:** Architected the three-layer relational data schema (Identity $\rightarrow$ Competition $\rightarrow$ Output) supporting complex live scoring cascades for national broadcasts aired on ESPN. 
* **Core Mechanics:** Consolidated real-time game states into a single component engine controlled by a singular `events.status` field. Engineered an advanced design system comprised of 8 distinct color ramps and 96 colors using custom contextual palettes (e.g., "Dink Night", "Match Point").
* **Case Study Strategy:** Employs a strict "V2 Before V1" reveal structure. Displays the highly polished, responsive web application first, followed by an honest analysis of the initial chaotic structural "fire drill" to emphasize system-level recovery and judgment.

### 2. BendixKing KX200 (Honeywell)
* **Role:** Safety-Critical Interaction Design Lead.
* **System Architecture:** Redesigned touch-screen aviation avionics UX operating under strict regulatory and hardware performance restrictions.
* **Core Mechanics:** Conducted deep embodied research (taking physical flying lessons) to evaluate user cognitive loads and physical constraints at 8,000 feet. Built custom low-latency hardware interaction prototypes and guided the interface through rigorous formal FAA validation protocols.

### 3. TokenProof
* **Role:** Product Design Lead.
* **System Architecture:** Designed a universal conditional access primitive, abstracting high-complexity token-gating mechanics into consumer-grade security tools.
* **Outcome:** Established the core transaction and verification logic that facilitated secure infrastructure scaling, leading directly to an acquisition by Yuga Labs in December 2024.

### 4. Handled / Snapmoves (Transparency by Design)
* **Role:** Product Design Lead across Consumer Trust, Operational Tooling, and Enterprise Configuration Tracks.
* **System Architecture:** Rebuilt the highly fragmented, opaque moving industry lifecycle from first principles by designing a centralized "Verified Inventory" primitive. 
* **Core Mechanics:** Engineered a multi-track architecture across 5 user roles (Customer, Coordinator, Sales Agent, Hand, Driver) that mapped real-time carrier data, localized service constraints, and automated camera capture fields across 95 markets and 112 physical agent locations.
* **Outcome:** Acquired by UniGroup to power the national software infrastructure of United Van Lines and Mayflower Transit.

### 5. NerdWallet Currency Design System
* **Role:** Systems Design Lead.
* **System Architecture:** Rebuilt NerdWallet's foundational internal design language system ("Currency") as an independent product ecosystem.
* **Core Mechanics:** Standardized components across the entire consumer platform surface. Led cross-functional adoption strategies and migration logic to convert fragmented engineering repos into a singular source of truth.

### 6. WFCA Wildfire Mapping
* **Role:** Principal Experience Designer.
* **System Architecture:** Designed crisis-management geospatial interfaces.
* **Core Mechanics:** Handled extreme data density feeds under strict connection constraints, treating a mobile-first, zero-latency viewport as a strict requirement for survival and property preservation rather than a visual trend.
