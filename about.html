We started as a two-person operation with a single conviction — that craft and speed are not mutually exclusive. Every project we take on is treated as if it were our own.

Today the studio spans three cities and a tight team of designers, developers and strategists who share the same obsession with detail and the same intolerance for mediocre work.

Lucas Martin

Founder & Creative Director

Lucas founded the studio with a simple rule: never ship something you wouldn't put your name on. He leads creative direction and stays close to every project, from first brief to final delivery.

Emma Müller 02
Emma Müller

Art Director

Emma shapes the visual language of the studio. With a background in editorial and brand design, she oversees art direction across all client work and keeps the aesthetic bar consistently high.

Olivia Smith 03
Olivia Smith

Senior Designer

Olivia came from a product design background and brought a sharper UX lens to the studio. She bridges visual ambition with functional clarity, leading design on the studio's most complex projects.

Mark Spencer 04
Mark Spencer

Front-End Developer

Mark is the developer who makes the animations actually work. He handles all front-end implementation with a particular obsession for performance, smooth interactions, and clean, maintainable code.

Charlotte Dunn 05
Charlotte Dunn

Brand Strategist

Charlotte manages strategy and client relationships. She translates complex briefs into clear creative direction and keeps projects coherent from the first conversation through to final handoff.

Rick Harrison 06

Motion Designer

Hannah Penn 07

Studio Manager
